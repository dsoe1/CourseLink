What is CourseLink?

CourseLink is an online textbook marketplace where you can buy textbooks or sell them to other university students near you!

Buy

Search and purchase materials from our collection of novels and textbooks, many of which are required course materials for your classes.

Sell

Need some extra cash? Consider using CourseLink to sell your used (or unused) course materials, to your fellow classmates!

Why CourseLink?

Using a third-party marketplace, or even your institution's own bookstore can be pricey, as they often mark-up the prices of their used textbooks. Here on CourseLink, the majority of the profits will go to the sellers, and the prices you pay are negotiable with the sellers on the website.
